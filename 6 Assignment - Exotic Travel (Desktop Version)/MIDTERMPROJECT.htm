Hot Destination this Summer

Florianópolis - Santa Catarina
Florianópolis, the capital of Santa Catarina state, is know throughout Brazil for its miles and miles of gourgeous beaches, excellent seafood and traditional Azorean fishing villages.

The downtown is in the mainland, but the best attractions (beaches!!) are in the island, wich can be easily accessed via public bridges, one of wich is Brazil’s largest suspension bridge. The island is a large beach community divided into three sections: north, south and east coasts. In addition to the bay and ocean beaches, the city features a large lagoon – Lagoa da Conceição.
